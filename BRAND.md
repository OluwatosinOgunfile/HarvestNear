# HarvestNearU Brand System

## Brand Idea

**Name:** HarvestNearU  
**Promise:** Fresh Local Produce, Found Here.  
**Supporting line:** Fresh starts here.

HarvestNearU connects consumers with trusted nearby farmers. The brand should feel local, capable, fresh, and practical rather than rustic or overly decorative.

## Wordmark

Write the name as **HarvestNearU**, with no space. The approved wordmark uses harvest gold for `Harvest` and field green for `Near`. The wheat-and-sun location pin is the primary symbol and may be used alone for compact application icons.

Maintain clear space around the lockup equal to at least half the symbol width. Do not stretch, rotate, outline, or place the wordmark over visually busy photography.

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary field | Deep green | `#1F5B3A` |
| Fresh accent | Field green | `#2D7650` |
| Harvest gold | Grain gold | `#C58D00` |
| Urgency and logistics | Warm orange | `#E9763D` |
| Primary ink | Forest ink | `#17231B` |
| Light surface | Field paper | `#F7F8F3` |
| Dark surface | Night field | `#111713` |

Green communicates trust and locality. Gold expresses harvest quality and sunlight. Lime remains a supporting freshness tint, while orange is used sparingly for delivery movement, urgency, and low-stock states.

## Typography

- **Brand and UI:** Arial/Helvetica system sans-serif, bold and direct.
- **Editorial headings:** Georgia, medium weight, for warmth and produce storytelling.
- **Operational text:** Compact sans-serif for prices, inventory, delivery, and order states.

Avoid oversized type inside dashboards and operational panels. Letter spacing remains neutral except for short uppercase labels.

## Voice

- Clear before clever.
- Warm, local, and specific.
- Focus on freshness, distance, farmer identity, and delivery certainty.
- Prefer “harvest”, “nearby farmer”, and “ready today” over generic ecommerce language.
- Never imply that all produce is organic unless a listing is verified as organic.

## Example Copy

- **Hero:** HarvestNearU. Fresh starts here.
- **Brand promise:** Fresh Local Produce, Found Here.
- **Marketplace:** Harvests near you.
- **Checkout:** Your produce is reserved for 10 minutes.
- **Farmer invitation:** Sell today's harvest to customers nearby.
- **Delivery:** From farm gate to your doorstep.

## Photography

Use bright, natural product photography that clearly shows the produce being sold. Favor real texture, crop-safe framing, soft daylight, and recognizable Nigerian market or farm context. Avoid dark stock photography, excessive styling, text overlays, and images that obscure product quality.
